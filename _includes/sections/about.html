How Do You Pronounce Cire?

We prefer to pronounce "CIRE" as "SIR-RAY" for a couple of reasons. Cire comes from an old French term for "Wax" and that's our main medium. Also, since Ray Cardillo is the artisan behind the brand, it's a clever way to incorporate his name into the brand.
